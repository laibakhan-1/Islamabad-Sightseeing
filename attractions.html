Top Attractions of Islamabad

Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque is the largest mosque in Pakistan, known for its unique and modern design. It is located at the foothills of the Margalla Hills.

  • Location: Sector F-5
  • Opening Hours: 9 AM - 7 PM
  • Entry Fees: Free
  • Travel Tips: Dress modestly and visit during early morning or evening for best photos.

Pakistan Monument

The Pakistan Monument symbolizes the four provinces and three territories of Pakistan. It is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike.

  • Location: Shakarparian Hills
  • Opening Hours: 9 AM - 5 PM
  • Entry Fees: PKR 20
  • Travel Tips: Best visited during sunset for amazing views and photos.
