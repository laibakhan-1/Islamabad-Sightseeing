Tour Itineraries

One-Day Tour of Islamabad

  • Morning: Visit Faisal Mosque and Daman-e-Koh for panoramic city views.
  • Lunch: Enjoy local cuisine at Melody Food Park.
  • Afternoon: Explore Pakistan Monument and Lok Virsa Museum.
  • Evening: Relax at Rawal Lake or shop at Centaurus Mall.

Weekend Getaway

  • Day 1: Explore Islamabad attractions and Margalla Hills hiking trails.
  • Day 2: Visit Saidpur Village, cultural shows, and try local food.

Islamabad + Murree Combo Trip

  • Day 1: Islamabad sightseeing.
  • Day 2: Travel to Murree, visit Mall Road and Patriata.
  • Day 3: Return to Islamabad or continue exploring.
